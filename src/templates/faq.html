FAQ

  1. First SignUp/Login to the application.
  2. Go to 'Upload CSV' and upload well-structured CSV file. (As described in 2nd FAQ)
  3. View list of product sales which are extracted from CSV, by going to 'Sales-List'.
  4. Then go to 'Home' to Analyze the sales data by using sales dates or by transaction ID.
  5. The chart and the option to Add Report are displayed at the end of 'Home' page.
  6. Go to 'Reports' to view reports in detail or in PDF.
  7. The Report PDFs can be downloaded for using offline.
